Off-Campus Use of Advance

Accessing the Advance system from off campus will require the use of the Junos Pulse VPN solution. Please follow the instructions here to set up and install Junos Pulse on your computer.

If you have any issues with the install and setup, please contact the Service Desk at 607-274-1000 or servicedesk@ithaca.edu for assistance and troubleshooting.

Junos Pulse is also available from the iOS App Store for Apple's mobile devices. If you need assistance connecting from your iPhone/iPad, please contact Benjamin Costello in Institutional Advancement.
